Given a string s consisting of some words separated by some number of spaces, return the length of the last word in the string. A word is a maximal substring consisting of non-space characters only. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = "Hello World" <br><h4>Output:</h4> 5 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> The last word is "World" with length 5. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = " fly me to the moon " <br><h4>Output:</h4> 4 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> The last word is "moon" with length 4. <br><h4>Example 3:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = "luffy is still joyboy" <br><h4>Output:</h4> 6 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> The last word is "joyboy" with length 6. <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= s.length <= 104 s consists of only English letters and spaces \' \'. There will be at least one word in s.
