You are given an integer array nums and you have to return a new counts array. The counts array has the property where counts[i] is the number of smaller elements to the right of nums[i]. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [5,2,6,1] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [2,1,1,0] <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> To the right of 5 there are 2 smaller elements (2 and 1). To the right of 2 there is only 1 smaller element (1). To the right of 6 there is 1 smaller element (1). To the right of 1 there is 0 smaller element. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [-1] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [0] <br><h4>Example 3:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [-1,-1] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [0,0] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= nums.length <= 105 -104 <= nums[i] <= 104
