Given an integer array nums and an integer k, return the k most frequent elements. You may return the answer in any order. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [1,1,1,2,2,3], k = 2 <br><h4>Output:</h4> [1,2] <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [1], k = 1 <br><h4>Output:</h4> [1] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= nums.length <= 105 k is in the range [1, the number of unique elements in the array]. It is guaranteed that the answer is unique. Follow up:</h4> Your algorithm's time complexity must be better than O(n log n), where n is the array's size.
