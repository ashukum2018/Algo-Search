Write a program to count the number of days between two dates. The two dates are given as strings, their format is YYYY-MM-DD as shown in the examples. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> date1 = "2019-06-29", date2 = "2019-06-30" <br><h4>Output:</h4> 1 <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> date1 = "2020-01-15", date2 = "2019-12-31" <br><h4>Output:</h4> 15 <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> The given dates are valid dates between the years 1971 and 2100.
