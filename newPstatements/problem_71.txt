You are climbing a staircase. It takes n steps to reach the top. Each time you can either climb 1 or 2 steps. In how many distinct ways can you climb to the top? <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> n = 2 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 2 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> There are two ways to climb to the top. 1. 1 step + 1 step 2. 2 steps <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> n = 3 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 3 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> There are three ways to climb to the top. 1. 1 step + 1 step + 1 step 2. 1 step + 2 steps 3. 2 steps + 1 step <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= n <= 45
