Given an integer number n, return the difference between the product of its digits and the sum of its digits. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> n = 234 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 15 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Product of digits = 2 * 3 * 4 = 24 Sum of digits = 2 + 3 + 4 = 9 Result = 24 - 9 = 15 <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> n = 4421 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 21 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Product of digits = 4 * 4 * 2 * 1 = 32 Sum of digits = 4 + 4 + 2 + 1 = 11 Result = 32 - 11 = 21 <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= n <= 10^5
