In a warehouse, there is a row of barcodes, where the ith barcode is barcodes[i]. Rearrange the barcodes so that no two adjacent barcodes are equal. You may return any answer, and it is guaranteed an answer exists. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> barcodes = [1,1,1,2,2,2] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [2,1,2,1,2,1] <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> barcodes = [1,1,1,1,2,2,3,3] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [1,3,1,3,1,2,1,2] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= barcodes.length <= 10000 1 <= barcodes[i] <= 10000
