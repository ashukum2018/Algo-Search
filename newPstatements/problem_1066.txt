Given two numbers, hour and minutes, return the smaller angle (in degrees) formed between the hour and the minute hand. Answers within 10-5 of the actual value will be accepted as correct. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> hour = 12, minutes = 30 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 165 <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> hour = 3, minutes = 30 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 75 <br><h4>Example 3:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> hour = 3, minutes = 15 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 7.5 <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= hour <= 12 0 <= minutes <= 59
