The power of the string is the maximum length of a non-empty substring that contains only one unique character. Given a string s, return the power of s. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = "leetcode" <br><h4>Output:</h4> 2 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> The substring "ee" is of length 2 with the character \'e\' only. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = "abbcccddddeeeeedcba" <br><h4>Output:</h4> 5 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> The substring "eeeee" is of length 5 with the character \'e\' only. <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= s.length <= 500 s consists of only lowercase English letters.
