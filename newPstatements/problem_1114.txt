There are 4 groups with largest size. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> n = 2 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 2 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> There are 2 groups [1], [2] of size 1. <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= n <= 104
