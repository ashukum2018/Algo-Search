Given an integer array nums, move all 0's to the end of it while maintaining the relative order of the non-zero elements. Note that you must do this in-place without making a copy of the array. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [0,1,0,3,12] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [1,3,12,0,0] <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [0] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [0] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= nums.length <= 104 -231 <= nums[i] <= 231 - 1 Follow up:</h4> Could you minimize the total number of operations done?
