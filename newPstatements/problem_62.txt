Given the head of a linked list, rotate the list to the right by k places. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> head = [1,2,3,4,5], k = 2 <br><h4>Output:</h4> [4,5,1,2,3] <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> head = [0,1,2], k = 4 <br><h4>Output:</h4> [2,0,1] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> The number of nodes in the list is in the range [0, 500]. -100 <= Node.val <= 100 0 <= k <= 2 * 109
