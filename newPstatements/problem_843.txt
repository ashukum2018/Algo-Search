Given an integer array nums and an integer k, modify the array in the following way:</h4> choose an index i and replace nums[i] with -nums[i]. You should apply this process exactly k times. You may choose the same index i multiple times. Return the largest possible sum of the array after modifying it in this way. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [4,2,3], k = 1 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 5 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Choose index 1 and nums becomes [4,-2,3]. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [3,-1,0,2], k = 3 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 6 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Choose indices (1, 2, 2) and nums becomes [3,1,0,2]. <br><h4>Example 3:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [2,-3,-1,5,-4], k = 2 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 13 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Choose indices (1, 4) and nums becomes [2,3,-1,5,4]. <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= nums.length <= 104 -100 <= nums[i] <= 100 1 <= k <= 104
