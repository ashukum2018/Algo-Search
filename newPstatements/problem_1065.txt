All other sub-arrays of size 3 have averages less than 4 (the threshold). <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> arr = [11,13,17,23,29,31,7,5,2,3], k = 3, threshold = 5 <br><h4>Output:</h4> 6 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> The first 6 sub-arrays of size 3 have averages greater than 5. Note that averages are not integers. <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= arr.length <= 105 1 <= arr[i] <= 104 1 <= k <= arr.length 0 <= threshold <= 104
