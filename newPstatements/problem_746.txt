Given an array of integers arr, find the sum of min(b), where b ranges over every (contiguous) subarray of arr. Since the answer may be large, return the answer modulo 109 + 7. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> arr = [3,1,2,4] <br><h4>Output:</h4> 17 <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Subarrays are [3], [1], [2], [4], [3,1], [1,2], [2,4], [3,1,2], [1,2,4], [3,1,2,4]. Minimums are 3, 1, 2, 4, 1, 1, 2, 1, 1, 1. Sum is 17. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> arr = [11,81,94,43,3] <br><h4>Output:</h4> 444 <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= arr.length <= 3 * 104 1 <= arr[i] <= 3 * 104
