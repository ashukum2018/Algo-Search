If there is more than one possible answer, return the longest word with the smallest lexicographical order. However, "apple" is lexicographically smaller than "apply". <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= words.length <= 1000 1 <= words[i].length <= 30 words[i] consists of lowercase English letters.
