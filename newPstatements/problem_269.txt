You must make sure your result is the smallest in lexicographical order among all possible results. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = "bcabc" <br><h4>Output:</h4> "abc" <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> s = "cbacdcbc" <br><h4>Output:</h4> "acdb" <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= s.length <= 104 s consists of lowercase English letters.
