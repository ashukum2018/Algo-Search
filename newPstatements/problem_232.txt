You are given an array of integers nums, there is a sliding window of size k which is moving from the very left of the array to the very right. You can only see the k numbers in the window. Each time the sliding window moves right by one position. Return the max sliding window. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [1,3,-1,-3,5,3,6,7], k = 3 <br><h4>Output:</h4> [3,3,5,5,6,7] <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> Window position Max --------------- ----- [1 3 -1] -3 5 3 6 7 3 1 [3 -1 -3] 5 3 6 7 3 1 3 [-1 -3 5] 3 6 7 5 1 3 -1 [-3 5 3] 6 7 5 1 3 -1 -3 [5 3 6] 7 6 1 3 -1 -3 5 [3 6 7] 7 <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> nums = [1], k = 1 <br><h4>Output:</h4> [1] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= nums.length <= 105 -104 <= nums[i] <= 104 1 <= k <= nums.length
