Given an array of string words. Return all strings in words which is substring of another word in any order. String words[i] is substring of words[j], if can be obtained removing some characters to left and/or right side of words[j]. <br><h4>Example 1:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> words = ["mass","as","hero","superhero"] <br><h4>Output:</h4> ["as","hero"] <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> "as" is substring of "mass" and "hero" is substring of "superhero". ["hero","as"] is also a valid answer. <br><h4>Example 2:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> words = ["leetcode","et","code"] <br><h4>Output:</h4> ["et","code"] <br><h4>Explanation:</h4> "et", "code" are substring of "leetcode". <br><h4>Example 3:</h4> <br><h4>Input:</h4> words = ["blue","green","bu"] <br><h4>Output:</h4> [] <br><h4>Constraints:</h4> 1 <= words.length <= 100 1 <= words[i].length <= 30 words[i] contains only lowercase English letters. It\'s guaranteed that words[i] will be unique.
